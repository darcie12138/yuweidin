2023 Chile GCC

Posted by Darcie on Fri, Nov 3, 2023

A journey I’d been anticipating for three years finally began after 30 hours of flight and two days of layovers. 一场我期待了3年的旅行，在30小时的航班和两天的辗转之后，终于开启。

Setting Off

Capturing the Chilean snow-capped mountains from the plane.

Moments with everyone at Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport.

Memories

I remember first seeing GCC in a Melton tweet: a fully-funded trip abroad, what an opportunity! I must admit, it was this thought that drew me to join Melton. Little did I know then how much Melton would change my college life, and I dare say, my life, in the next three years. 想起当时第一次在Melton的推文上看到GCC：全额资助的出国旅行，这么好的机会？不得不承认我是冲着这个念头加入的Melton。那时候的我还没有意识到在未来的三年，Melton会给我的大学生活，甚至我的人生我愿意说，带来多大的变化。

Even before disembarking, I saw messages from Chilean fellows on WhatsApp about airport pickups. The sight of enthusiastic Cami and somewhat shy Gloria swept away all fatigue from the journey. I suspected Gloria to be an introvert. Little did I expect her to drag ZU’s bunny from bed for a midnight dance, perhaps that’s the South American spirit. 还没下飞机就在WhatsApp上看到智利fellow发来在机场接机的消息，直到看到热情的Cami和有点害羞的Gloria，旅行的疲惫一扫而空。Gloria肯定是i人，我心想。没想到她会半夜把ZU的兔子从床上拉起来去蹦迪，或许这就是南美人吧。

Opening Ceremony

Actually, I hardly felt it real until the Opening Ceremony the next day, as jet lag had me in a primordial haze. The afternoon dance thoroughly woke me up, witnessing Eason’s soulful dance moves, a lifetime’s satisfaction. It was probably the first time I danced so freely in public, without any rehearsal, just dancing however I felt like. South America really does help one relax. 其实直到第二天Opening Ceremony的时候都没什么实感，因为时差整个人处于盘古开天地的混沌之中。下午的舞蹈彻底把我唤醒了，有生之年看到了Eason的灵魂舞步，满足。这几乎是我从小到大第一次在公共场合这么自由地跳舞，没有任何排练，想跳成什么样就跳成什么样，南美真的会让人放松。

My Friends

With my friends

I often say I am both an idealist and a timid soul confined by reality. But in Chile, it seemed I found the courage to face much more. 我总说我是一个既理想主义，又囿于现实无法逃脱的胆小鬼。但是在智利，好像有了勇气去面对很多。

Seizing every opportunity to interact with fellows from various countries

Recalling this dream that escaped the narrow confines of college life, I’m always struck by seemingly overlooked details: the free birds in Joaquin’s yard, Pedro who was sad thinking he lost an envelope, and the Chilean guy who thought all Chinese knew Kungfu… So many little things. What I find most difficult to let go of from GCC are these friends from five continents, each kind, vivid, and brave. I saw endless possibilities in them. I’m grateful for the night I decided to fill out the Melton application, allowing me to see more opportunities on the other side of the world. 对于这场从狭窄的大学生活里逃出来的梦，回忆的时候总是蹦出来一些好像被忽略的小细节：Joaquin院子里自由的鸟、cheer up以为自己丢了信封而很伤心的Pedro、还有以为中国人都会Chinese Kungfu的智利小哥……太多太多小事。GCC最难割舍的是这群来自五个大洲的朋友们，每一个人都善良、鲜活、勇敢，我在他们身上看到了无限的可能性。很感谢那天晚上的自己下定决心填了Melton的报名表，给我在地球另一端看见更多的机会。

I’ll forever remember the spring in South America, the mountains, and the adorable fellows. 我会永远记得南美的春天、雪山和可爱的fellows.
